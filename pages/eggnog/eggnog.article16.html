my famous sausage soup recipe -- May 14, 2026

♪Tea For Two - Blossom Dearie♪

I made this soup after losing a 2v1 clutch to win a counter strike match on cache. we tied 12-12.

serving size: 2 cups
  1. sweat half a yellow onion in 1 tablespoon of olive oil. add salt and pepper
  2. add 2-4 frozen sausages. i don't know what type of sausage it was, but it tasted like maple syrup.
  3. once onions are translucent, add 2 cups of beef broth
  4. cook for 15-20 minutes or until the sausage is cooked through
  5. add 1-2 tablespoons of Worchester sauce
  6. add paprika to taste
  7. serve with crackers
